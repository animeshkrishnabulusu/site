A site rebuild

another year another rebuild

Feb 16, 2025 

I am rebuilding my blog once again. This time it is a significant effort involving writing meaningfully, and building a new static site generator.

Writing well about a subject of interest is hard. There is no alternate for working out a subject on the ground to depth. To change how I approach writing, I am focusing on writing a draft twice a week as a first step.

I used Lektor for generating my site from 2016 onwards. I loved how simpler it is than most other popular ones like Hugo and Eleventy. I chose to build a custom static site generator with spiritual compatibility to Lektor. I will write about it once I work out enough details. Until I build that static site generator, I will write my posts in raw HTML.
